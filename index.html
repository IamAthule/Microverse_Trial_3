Introduction

A smart TV, or connected TV, is a regular television that comes with built-in internet and interactive Web 2.0 functions that enable users to stream music and videos, browse the internet, and view photos. Essentially, smart TVs combine the functions of computers, televisions, and digital media players into one technological device.
